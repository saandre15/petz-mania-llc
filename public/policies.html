Petz Mania Requirements and Policies

Effective as of June 1st, 2019

Dog and Cat Requirements

1.1 Behavior

  • Well Socialized
  • Well Behaved

1.2 Spayed / Neutered

  • Female dog or cat must be neutered
  • Male dog or cat must be neutered
  • Dog bellow 6 months, do not have to spayed or neutered
  • Cat bellow 8 weeks, do not have to spayed or neutered

1.3 Vaccinations

  • Dog must have rabies, distemper, and bordatella vaccination that are up to date
  • Cat must have rabies, distemper, bordetella, and FPV vaccination that are up to date
  • Exceptions are dog/cat with weaken immune or allergy to vaccination

1.4 Nail Length

  • Dog nails should be trimmed, as they could be dangerous in the play area
  • Petz Mania offer nail trims

1.5 Fleas and Ticks

  • Dog and Cat without flea and tick
  • Petz Mania offer flea and tick bath

Store Policy

2.1 Hours of Operation

Mon - Fri 7:00am – 7:00pm
Saturday 7:30am – 7:00pm
Sunday CLOSED

2.2 Holiday Hours

During the major holidays, our lobby will be close for grooming, self-serve bath and daycare. As always, even when our lobby is closed, our staff will be taking great care of your pup if he or she is boarding. Petz Mania’s lobby will be close during the following major holidays:

Drop-off/ Pick up for boarding only (Holiday): 9:00am to 12:00pm

  • Easter Sunday
  • Memorial Day
  • July 4th Day
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving
  • Christmas Day
  • New Year’s Day

2.3 Security

Dogs entering or exiting our facility must be on leash.

For security reason, proper photo identification may be requested to release dog.

2.4 Payment and Refunds

A valid credit card must be put on file during check in.

Day Care and boarding prepaid packages are non-refundable but the unused balance can be used to purchase other services or merchandise offered in our store.

2.5 Food and Medications

We encourage owner to provide their own dog food. Please bag the food in sealed sandwich bag with labels by meal.

For additional charge, we provide Hill’s Science Diet Dog food $1.50 per meal for small dogs below 15 lbs and $2.50 for other dogs. Please let us know how often you want us to feed your dog.

We would be happy to administer oral and topical medicine for your dogs for free but please label them with instructions in its original package including the dog’s name, dosage, schedule, and method. First time day care and boarding clients must submit a signed service agreement.

Please let us know if you dog eats within one hour prior to coming to our day care. This is to prevent bloat which can be caused by playing to quickly after eating.

2.6 Tours and Temperament

Tours are welcome.

Dogs must be able to pass the temperament test prior to admittance. Please schedule a meet and greet prior to admittance. Temperament testing is to find our if your dog can fit in our play group.

2.7 Unacceptable boarding and daycare breed

Due to the communal nature of our boarding/daycare environment we cannot accept the following breeds:

  • Pit-Bull
  • Stafford Shire Terrier
  • American Bulldog
  • Cane Corso
  • Dogo Argentino
  • Dogo Argentino
  • Wolf-Hybrid

2.8 Late Pick-Up Fee

There be a late pick up fee of $10 per 15 minutes after stated close time. (Subject to availability)
